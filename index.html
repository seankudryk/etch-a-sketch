Please enter the dimensions of of your canvas above, then click the "Set Grid" button.

For example: a value of "40" will create a grid of 40 pixels x 40 pixels. The canvas size will not change.

Use Your Keyboard to Shade Your Creation: Hold CTRL and hover over a pixel to make its color LIGHTER Hold SHIFT and hover over a pixel to make its color DARKER Hold ALT(Windows) or OPTION(Mac) and hover over a pixel to reset its darkness to the default value Opacity will stay the same for each pixel even if you switch colors - this allows you to experiment with different colors if you're happy with your shading, but unhappy with the color profile
